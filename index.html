The AR effect we created for Barba Norteña was used by thousands of users just on the first day.

Barba Norteña - Classic

This is an AR effect I created in memory of the total eclipse of the sun that was seen in parts of North America this past April 8th, 2024.

Technical details:

  • Use of a custom script to manage the Hand Tracking functionality in order to detect when a hand is located near or in the same position as the 3D Sun.
  • Use of canvas and texture extraction to change the background color of the scene.
